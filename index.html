Alina Ruff's CSCE 190 Site

Problem Statement: Event Check-in

Our users are frustrated and they need a faster way to check into conferences with a larger audience. Our solution is aimed to provide the user with a seamless and easy check-in for events.

Affinity Diagram: Event Check-in

My group and I worked together to brainstorm all things that are associated with modifying our event check-in. We created 6 categories that we feel were most important for our topic: Security, Check-in, Staffing, Speed, Products, and In App Stats.

4 Personas: Event Check-in

My group and I worked together to create 4 different personas that will use our event check in. We created a college student, a 32 year old woman, a middle aged woman and an elderly man.

4 Storyboards: Event Check-in

My group and I worked together to create 4 different storyboards that will tell a story of our personnas using an app to improve event check-ins. Each of the personnas storyboard's share a different narrative.

Sketches: Event Check-in

My group and I worked together to create 4 different sketches of an app. The sketches show ideas that we created for an app that uses advance check-in for career events.

Paper Prototype: Event Check-in

I created a paper prototype of an app that allows you to check in prior to career events located around you.

Usability Testing: Event Check-in

I tested my prototype on 2 different users and gathered feedback on what I can improve on.

Low Fi Prototype: Event Check-in

My team and I created a low fidelity prototype using proto.io. We created a rough draft of what we want our even check-in app to look like.

High Fi Prototype: Event Check-in

My team and I created a high fidelity prototype using proto.io. We created our final draft of what we want our even check-in app to look like. Last week we created our rough draft and now we added a few extra interactions to finalize our project.
